About This Calculator

The Heart Attack Risk Calculator is designed to help healthcare professionals and individuals estimate the risk of developing cardiovascular disease based on established risk factors. The tool is based on widely accepted medical guidelines and is intended for educational and informational purposes only.

Who Should Use This Tool?

  • Healthcare professionals for patient education and risk assessment
  • Individuals interested in understanding their cardiovascular risk
